
# **Core Skills & Wills Matrix — Skill Definitions**

Aetherion tracks a curated set of skills and wills that reflect the company’s identity:
**fast prototyping, clear thinking, cross-disciplinary collaboration, and human-centered AI.**

Each skill has a normalized **identifier** and a descriptive **full name**.

---

## **Technical Skills**

### **`ml_engineering` — Machine Learning Engineering**

Ability to design, train, optimize, and validate ML models using modern frameworks and best practices.

### **`llm_nlp` — LLM & NLP Engineering**

Skills related to large language models, prompt design, evaluation methods, and NLP pipelines.

### **`cv_engineering` — Computer Vision Engineering**

Working with image/video data, vision models, data labeling workflows, and edge/deployment optimizations.

### **`edge_ai` — Edge AI & Embedded Deployment**

Deploying AI models on constrained devices (e.g., Jetson), optimizing hardware/software integration, handling physical-world constraints.

### **`software_engineering` — Software Engineering**

Building maintainable applications, APIs, dashboards, services, and integrating AI components into usable systems.

### **`mlops_infra` — MLOps & Infrastructure**

Designing lightweight but reliable deployment pipelines, CI/CD for AI prototypes, observability for PoCs.

### **`data_engineering` — Data Engineering & Pipelines**

Building ingestion, transformation, validation, and mobility layers for training and inference.

---

## **Consulting & Customer Skills**

### **`workshop_facilitation` — Workshop Facilitation**

Leading structured, clear, and outcome-driven discussions with customers; extracting insights.

### **`requirements_design` — Requirements Design & Scoping**

Translating business needs into technical goals, clarifying feasibility, capturing constraints.

### **`stakeholder_alignment` — Stakeholder Alignment & Communication**

Managing expectations, resolving ambiguity, and keeping diverse parties aligned.

### **`narrative_building` — Narrative Building & Explanation**

Crafting clear, grounded stories around technical work; explaining AI to non-technical audiences.

---

## **Collaboration & Team Skills**

### **`cross_functional_collab` — Cross-Functional Collaboration**

Ability to work fluidly across AI, software, and consulting boundaries.

### **`knowledge_sharing` — Knowledge Sharing & Documentation**

Capturing lessons, writing clear notes, surfacing insights, and reducing single points of failure.

### **`mentoring_support` — Mentoring & Technical Support**

Supporting teammates, reviewing work, and helping people grow in small-team environments.

### **`risk_awareness` — Risk Awareness & Responsible Ownership**

Recognizing system, workload, and delivery risks; escalating early; shaping sustainable solutions.

---

## **Personal & Cultural Skills**

### **`clarity_of_thought` — Clarity of Thought**

Ability to simplify complexity, articulate trade-offs, and avoid jargon without losing precision.

### **`calm_under_uncertainty` — Calm Under Uncertainty**

Maintaining composure during live demos, ambiguous scoping, or unexpected system behavior.

### **`experimentation_mindset` — Experimentation Mindset**

Trying ideas quickly, embracing failure as learning, showing “ugly prototypes” without hesitation.

### **`ethical_judgment` — Ethical Judgment & Human-Centered Thinking**

Ensuring AI impacts are understandable, beneficial, and aligned with real human workflows.
